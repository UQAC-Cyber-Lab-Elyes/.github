# UQAC Cyber Lab - Elyes

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for the **Cybersecurity & AI Research Lab** at the Université du Québec à Chicoutimi (UQAC), led by **Prof. Elyes**.

## 🚀 About the Lab
This organization serves as a collaborative hub for:
* **Academic Innovation:** Exploring the intersection of Explainable AI (XAI) and Cybersecurity.
* **Student Excellence:** Hosting and forking outstanding projects developed by UQAC students.
* **Open Research:** Sharing tools, CTF challenges, and research prototypes.

## 🎓 For Students
If your project has been forked here, congratulations! It has been recognized for its quality or research potential. 
- Please ensure your individual project READMEs are up to date.
- Use this as a portfolio piece for your future career in Cyber/AI.
